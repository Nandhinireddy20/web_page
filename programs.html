Our Programs

Program 1: Feed the Future

Feed the Future focuses on supporting smallholder farmers, promoting sustainable agriculture, improving nutrition, and building resilience to climate change in target countries.

Program 2: Farm to Market Alliance

The program helps farmers access finance, technology, and markets to increase their productivity and income.

Program 3: Global Agriculture and Food Security Program (GAFSP)

A multi-donor trust fund established by the World Bank to support agricultural development and food security projects in low-income countries. GAFSP provides financing for investments in agriculture, rural development, and nutrition.

Program 4: UNICEF's Nutrition Programs

UNICEF implements nutrition programs worldwide aimed at preventing and treating malnutrition among children and mothers. These programs include breastfeeding promotion, micronutrient supplementation, and community-based nutrition interventions
